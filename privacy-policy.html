Privacy Policy – Cookup

1. Introduction

Cuisine IA (“the Application”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our Android application, in compliance with Google OAuth, Google Play policies, and applicable data protection regulations.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Authentication Data (Google Sign-In)

When you sign in using Google OAuth, we may collect the following information provided by Google:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Google user identifier (OAuth ID)

Authentication is handled securely via Google OAuth 2.0.

2.2 User-Provided Data

The Application may collect data that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Ingredients entered by the user
  • Dietary preferences (allergies, cuisine types, language preferences)
  • Generated, saved, or favorite recipes

2.3 Technical Data

To ensure proper operation of the Application, we may collect:

  • Authentication tokens
  • Application usage data strictly necessary for functionality
  • Non-identifying technical device information

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate users securely
  • Provide and personalize application features
  • Save user preferences and recipes
  • Improve application stability and performance

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data for advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Personal data is stored securely on protected servers
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely on the device
  • Industry-standard security measures are used to prevent unauthorized access

Only authorized systems can access user data.

5. Data Sharing

We only share data in the following cases:

  • With Google, as part of the Google OAuth authentication process
  • When required by law or legal obligations

We do not share data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

6. Data Retention

User data is retained only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide application functionality
  • Comply with legal obligations

Users may request deletion of their data at any time.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the email address below.

8. Children’s Privacy

Cuisine IA is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.
